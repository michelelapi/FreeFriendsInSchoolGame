# Free Friends In School Game

A top-down Android adventure game where you play as a free kid navigating through school environments to rescue friends from teachers. Avoid capture, answer questions when caught, and progress through educational levels from elementary school all the way to PhD!

## üéÆ Game Overview

**Free Friends In School Game** is an educational action-adventure game where you navigate through school hallways, rescue your friends, avoid teachers, and test your knowledge with questions that scale with your progress.

### Core Gameplay

- **Rescue Friends**: Navigate the school environment and touch friends to rescue them
- **Avoid Teachers**: Steer clear of patrolling teachers - if caught, you must answer a question correctly to continue
- **Answer Questions**: When caught by a teacher, answer educational questions based on your current grade level
- **Progress Through Levels**: Advance from Elementary School ‚Üí Middle School ‚Üí High School ‚Üí College ‚Üí PhD
- **Score System**: Earn points based on friends rescued, time taken, and difficulty level

## üéì Educational Levels

The game progresses through five educational stages, each with increasing difficulty:

1. **Elementary School** (Grades 1-5): Simple math and basic questions
2. **Middle School** (Grades 6-8): Intermediate questions covering various subjects
3. **High School** (Grades 9-12): Subject-specific questions requiring deeper knowledge
4. **College**: Advanced questions testing critical thinking
5. **PhD**: Expert-level questions for the ultimate challenge

Each level features:

- Increasing difficulty with more teachers patrolling
- More friends to rescue
- Questions that match the educational stage
- Higher score multipliers for successful completion

## üéÆ How to Play

### Controls

- **Joystick**: Use the on-screen joystick to move your character in 4 directions (up, down, left, right)
- **Touch Friends**: Touch friends to rescue them and add to your score
- **Answer Questions**: When caught by a teacher, select the correct answer to continue playing

### Gameplay Tips

- Plan your route to avoid teachers while reaching friends
- Answer questions quickly to minimize time penalties
- Complete levels faster to earn time bonuses
- Progress through levels to unlock higher difficulty challenges

## üìä Scoring System

Your score is calculated based on multiple factors:

- **Base Points**: Points earned for each friend rescued
- **Time Bonus**: Complete levels faster to earn additional points
- **Difficulty Multiplier**: Higher educational levels provide score multipliers
- **Perfect Runs**: Rescue all friends without being caught for bonus points

Your high scores are automatically saved, so you can always try to beat your best performance!
